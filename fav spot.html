Tourist Spots in Pakistan

Here are my Fvorite breathtaking spots of Pakistan.

Naltar Valley

Located in Gilgit-Baltistan, Naltar Valley is known for its stunning lakes and scenic beauty. It is a popular spot for skiing and hiking.

Neelum Valley

Located in Azad Kashmir, Neelum Valley is known for its breathtaking landscapes, crystal clear streams, and stunning waterfalls.

Swat Valley

Located in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Swat Valley is known for its lush green hills, picturesque scenery, and rich cultural heritage.

Mohenjo-daro

Located in Sindh, Mohenjo-daro is an archaeological site that dates back to the Indus Valley Civilization. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-visit for history buffs.

Hunza Valley

Located in Gilgit-Baltistan, Hunza Valley is known for its stunning views of the surrounding mountains and its rich cultural heritage.

Khunjerab Pass

Located on the border between Pakistan and China, Khunjerab Pass is one of the highest paved international border crossings in the world. It offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and is a popular spot for photographers.
